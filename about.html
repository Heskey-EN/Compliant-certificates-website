About Compliant Property Certificates

Property compliance, made simple. Here's who we are, what we believe, and the standards behind every certificate we issue.

Our Story

Founded to make property compliance simple

Getting an EPC, EICR or Gas Safety certificate shouldn't mean chasing quotes, deciphering jargon, or wondering whether the price will change once the assessor turns up. We started Compliant Property Certificates to fix that.

We work with a network of accredited assessors, registered electricians and Gas Safe registered engineers across England, Wales and Scotland. Whether you're a homeowner, landlord, letting agent or business, you can book online, get a fixed price upfront, and receive your certificate digitally — usually within 24 hours of your appointment.

What We Believe

The way we work

Transparency

Compliance you can rely on
